Why save nature?

Preserving nature is crucial for biodiversity, clean air and water, climate regulation, and human well-being. It provides resources, supports the economy, and holds cultural and aesthetic value. Conservation ensures a sustainable future for diverse ecosystems and benefits for generations to come.
